This project follows the export of "nature-based solutions" from international policy frameworks into specific landscapes across Asia, Africa, and Latin America, with a focus on how Chinese actors shape that travel.

It asks who benefits, who bears the costs, and what gets lost when ecological complexity is translated into investable interventions.
